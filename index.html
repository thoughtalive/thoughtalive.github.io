A relational approach grows from inside and is a call for co-emergence and interdependence (Goodman 2018, 44)               where a multiplicity of non-linear flows overlap and move into play with no particular endpoint.

                      / Research-creation is approached as an active hyphen that brings making to thinking and thinking to making (Manning 2016, 13).

How can generative processes and improvisation through live coding strengthen the potential of newways of coming into relation in the digital space?     


    How complex relational environments can be crafted     through writing algorithms?


How can the writing of algorithms tion allowing us give life to a relational experience where unpredictable movements of thought and sensation transit between given potentials and unknown events?

               / How can we sense the currents of the relational field?                      


        How live coding and algorithmic processes can enable the emergence of a non-linear process of thought to be experienced?              




          The force of change that activates the event works in the mode of speculative pragmatism, bringing with it an aspect of discovery, as it pushes the work to


         its overflow and this feedback into the process, so that it allows us to touch on the ineffable         quality of its more-than. This makes it speculative.     


    Speculative pragmatism, speculative in the sense that the process remains open to the more-than,


and pragmatic in the sense that it is completely invested in its 'something doing.' (Massumi 2016, 33).     


         The interstice of art and philosophy that unfolds into various propositions and events through my research-creation,                embrace speculative pragmatism as a technique that redirects its tendencies and helps keep it in movement.
